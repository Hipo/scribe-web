How ?

  • First select something on your Mac: Some text, a phone number, a URL, or a small image…
     

  • …and just press “Command + Shift + X”
     
     
     

  • The item appears instantly on your iOS device via low-energy Bluetooth. You don’t need Wi-Fi, or anything other than your two devices.

  • As soon as you slide to enter the app, Scribe copies the item automatically. You can also swipe left on any item to copy it.

  • It’s easy to see what you’ve sent so far. Tap or swipe right on different items to see all the ways you can interact with them.

  • Bluetooth LE

    Revolutionary Bluetooth LE technology is designed to be fast and use very little power, so it doesn't affect your battery life.

  • Always connected

    Your phone does not need an internet connection, nor does it need to be in the same Wi-Fi network as your Mac. To use Scribe, you just need your two devices, nothing more.

  • Reliable Security

    Everything sent through Scribe is secure, since it does not use a network connection. Simply a direct Bluetooth hotline between your devices; that’s the beauty of Scribe!

  • Works in the background

    Everything you send to your iPhone gets copied automatically. You don’t need to launch Scribe, or even switch apps so you can just paste. Of course, you can always launch Scribe to see your transfer history.
